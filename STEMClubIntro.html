Recursion 1

A recursive function

In division a quotient can be derived by subtracting a divisor from the dividend repeatedly and counting these subtraction steps until the divisor is greater than the remaining dividend.1 \begin{align*} \text{quotient}\;\left\{ \quad \begin{array}{l} \underline{1} & ← \text{dividend or numerator} \\[-5pt] 2 & ← \text{divisor or denominator} \end{array} \right. \end{align*}
